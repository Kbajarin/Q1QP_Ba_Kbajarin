Background

"Your grades are who you are."

The classic Pisay scholar keeps a watchful eye on his grades. This mindset of the grading system is present in a lot of students within the campus, which serves as an unhealthy perspective on their career. Has this taken a toll upon them?

In this study, we will determine the relationship between their academic performance to their own self-value to see if academic pressure has affected in their own human life.
